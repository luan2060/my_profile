About Me

Hi! I'm Vu Thanh Luan, a second-year Information Technology student with a clear goal: becoming a professional Fullstack Developer.

My coding journey began with C/C++ in my first year. As I dug deeper, I discovered my real passion lies in building web applications that serve real users.
